Guacamole Recipe:

Ingredient List:

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 of a bunch of cilantro
  • 1/3 onion chopped
  • 1/3 tomato chopped

    • Instructions:

    1. First, scoop the avocados out of their peel and mash them using a mortar and pestol.
    2. When the avocado is smooth enough for your liking, add in the lime juice and salt.
    3. Finally, add in the chopped cilantro, onion, and tomato.
    4. Enjoy with tortilla chips or nachos.
