Tzatziki Recipe

Ingredient List:

  • 1/2 cup grated cucumber
  • 1 cup whokle milk Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

    • Instructions:

    1. First, Dry the grated cucumber with a towel.
    2. Next, combine the yogurt, lemon juic, garlic, and salt.
    3. Finally, add in the cucmber and sprinkle with olive oil.
    4. Enjoy with pita bread or fresh vegetables.
