chili oil wonton recipe

Ingredient List:

  • 1/3 cup chili oil flakes
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 3 or 4 scallions or green onions
  • a quarter of a bundle of cilantro

Instructions:

  1. First, chop the cilantro and green onions.
  2. Next, measure the ingredients and combine in a small bowl.
  3. Finally, enjoy with fresh wontons or use to aid in flavor.
